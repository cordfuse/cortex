# Disclaimer

Please read this before using Cortex.

---

## What Cortex is

Cortex is a personal record-keeping protocol. It gives you a structured, private, git-based system for logging thoughts, experiences, and mental health information — with an AI that acts as a scribe and sounding board.

That is all it is.

---

## What Cortex is not

**Cortex is not therapy.** Nothing in this repository, and nothing the AI scribe says, constitutes mental health treatment, counselling, or clinical care.

**Cortex is not a diagnostic tool.** The AI does not assess, diagnose, or evaluate your mental health. Any patterns it reflects back to you are observations, not clinical judgements.

**Cortex is not a crisis service.** If you are in crisis, Cortex is not the right tool. See the crisis resources below.

**Cortex is not a substitute for professional care.** If you are struggling, please talk to a qualified mental health professional. A record-keeping protocol is not a replacement for a therapist, psychiatrist, GP, or counsellor.

---

## Your data

Cortex stores everything in your own private git repository. No data is sent to Cordfuse. No data is collected or retained by this protocol.

Your records are processed by the AI agent you choose to use (Claude, Gemini, OpenAI, etc.) under that platform's own privacy policy. Review your AI provider's data handling practices before recording sensitive information.

Cordfuse has no access to your records and accepts no liability for how you use this protocol or what you record in it.

## If you are in crisis

If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, suicidal thoughts, or feel you may harm yourself or others — stop and reach out now:

| Resource | Contact |
|---|---|
| **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** (US) | Call or text **988** |
| **Crisis Text Line** (US/CA) | Text HOME to **741741** |
| **Talk Suicide Canada** | Call **1-833-456-4566** · Text **45645** |
| **Samaritans** (UK/Ireland) | Call **116 123** |
| **Lifeline** (Australia) | Call **13 11 14** |
| **International directory** | [findahelpline.com](https://findahelpline.com) |

If you are in immediate danger, call your local emergency services.
